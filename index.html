Visit PortSaid

Enjoy the sea breeze and architecture in this historic district at the gateway of Egypt.

Top three activities to do at PortSaid

PortSaid Lighthouse

The world's first concrete structure at the crossroads of history and navigation.

The Ferry Ride

Take a free trip between two continents! The ferry connects Port Said (Africa) to Port Fuad (Asia) in a lovely journey across the Suez Canal.

The Royal Ferial Garden

Walk where kings and queens once stood. A historic lush garden overlooking the canal, perfect for a relaxing sunset walk.
